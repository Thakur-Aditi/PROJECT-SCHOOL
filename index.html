SAINT BRIJ MOHAN LAL SR. SEC. SCHOOL

ANANGPUR , FARIDABAD

Saint Brijmohan Lal Sr. Sec. School was founded by His Holiness Param Sant Mahatma Yashpal Ji Maharaj on 13th May 1985. He founded this school with the sole aim of imparting quality education in such a manner that a foundation for all round development (physical, moral, spiritual) of children is laid and they emerge as responsible citizen. The school has a team of well qualified, dedicated and hard working, result-oriented teachers for whom ‘Work is Worship’. The school believes in providing an atmosphere, which is highly conducive for an excellent learning and development of various skills as per the aptitude of students. There is an emphasis on development of divine qualities amongst students viz. humility, compassion, generosity, fearlessness, selfless service, patriotism, hardwork with determination, respect for elders and love for fellow beings. St. Brijmohan lal Senior Secondary School aflliated to CBSE , New Delhi is located in lush green Aravali Hills , south of Delhi near Historically famous village Anangpur in industrial township of Faridabad (Haryana) Established in year 1985 Noc no. – 21/08/2004s-1(4) obtained on 27/09/2005 School is recognised by Director, Sr Sec Education , Chanidagh (Haryana) School is provisionally affliated(Regular) to Central Board of Secondary Education since 1st apr 2005 upto 31st mar 2018, Affiliation Number – 530484 and Board since 2004. School is associated with Trust A.B.S.S(Regd.) SAINT BRIJMOHAN LAL SR. SEC. SCHOOL V.P.O. – ANANGPUR, DISTT FARIDABAD, HARYANA 121003. TEL: 0129-2515307
